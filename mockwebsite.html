Our Story

Crafting Community, One Cup at a Time

Founded in 2021 on Gloucester Road, The Daily Grind started with a simple mission: to serve independent specialty coffee without the pretension. We micro-roast our beans in small batches right here in Bristol, highlighting the unique flavour profiles of each origin.

We work directly with ethical smallholder farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Peru, ensuring every bean we source supports sustainable agricultural communities. Whether you're grabbing your morning flat white or settling in for a slow Saturday filter coffee, you're part of our journey.

Transparent Sustainability Metrics

We believe great coffee shouldn't leave an opaque footprints. Every batch of single-origin coffee bean can be tracked back directly to its originating cooperative estate.

We supply reusable coffee bags for our wholesale customers, reward dynamic discounts for keep-cup drop-ins, and recycle all organic coffee waste grounds into local community garden fertilizers across Bristol.

Menu & Sourcing FAQs

Do you offer dairy alternatives?

Yes, absolutely. We offer organic oat, almond, and soy alternatives. We source our oat milk from Minor Figures, engineered to complement microfoam architecture perfectly without masking underlying roasting traits.

Are your bakery products gluten-free?

We offer rotating gluten-free treats daily, including fudge brownies and almond cakes. However, since our partner bakeries utilize traditional flour, cross-contamination paths might exist.

Can I buy your whole beans to grind at home?

Yes! We sell retail bags (250g and 1kg) of our seasonal House Blend and rotating Single Origins directly over the bar. Our baristas can grind them to your specific brew method setup if needed.
